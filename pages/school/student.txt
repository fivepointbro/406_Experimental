subtitle: 406 (M) OTS Joining Instructions


text: 406 (M) OTS conducts training from two buildings at 12 Wing Shearwater: the Fumerton and Bing Training Center (FBTC) and 5 Building (lower base).


subtitle: Students Course Critques


text: Feedback is needed to evaluate the design, development and conduct of training, as well as to continually improve our training. Students will be emailed a link to a questionnaire at the appropriate time.  The feedback will then be analysed by 406 Standards personnel and the QAO who will then forward the results to other 406 personnel for information or appropriate action.
